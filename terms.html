2. Service Description

Receipt SN is an advanced, AI-powered receipt digitization and financial management tool designed to help individuals and organizations efficiently capture, store, categorize, and analyze receipt data for personal financial management purposes. The Service leverages cutting-edge artificial intelligence and machine learning technologies, including optical character recognition (OCR), natural language processing (NLP), and computer vision algorithms, to automatically extract, interpret, and structure information from physical and digital receipts.
