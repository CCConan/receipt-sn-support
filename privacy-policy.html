Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Thank you for using Receipt SN. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our iOS application ("App").

1. Information We Collect

Receipt Images & Data: When you scan receipts, the images and extracted data (date, merchant, amount, items) are stored locally on your device in a private sandbox. We do not have access to this data.

Subscription & Billing: All payment processing is handled securely by Apple through the App Store. We do not store credit card numbers or billing details on our servers.

App Usage Data: We may collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics to improve the App. This data cannot identify you personally.

Device Information: We may collect basic device information (model, iOS version) for crash reporting and app optimisation purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide receipt scanning and storage services on your device
  • To process subscription purchases and manage your account through Apple
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs (anonymous data only)
  • To provide customer support via email

3. Data Storage & Security

Local Storage: All receipt images and personal data are stored in your device's private sandbox. You can delete this data at any time by deleting the app or clearing the data within Settings.

Server Processing: When you use the AI-powered receipt analysis feature, images are temporarily sent to our secure backend (powered by Google Gemini) for processing. Data is processed in memory and is not stored permanently on our servers.

Encryption: Data in transit uses TLS 1.2+ encryption. Data at rest on your device uses iOS's built-in encryption.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Apple (App Store / StoreKit): Handles all subscription payments. Their privacy policy applies to payment processing.
  • Google Gemini AI: Used for receipt text extraction. Data is processed temporarily and not retained.
  • iCloud Sync: If you enable iCloud sync, data is stored in your personal iCloud container with end-to-end encryption. Only you can access this data.

5. AI & On-Device Processing

Local AI Mode: If you download the optional offline AI package, all receipt analysis is performed entirely on your device. No data leaves your device.

Cloud AI Mode: Receipt images are temporarily sent to our backend for analysis. Images are processed in volatile memory and are not stored on our servers after processing.

We Will Never:

  • Use your receipt data to train AI models
  • Sell or share your personal financial information to third parties
  • Access your receipt content without your explicit consent

6. Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have full control:

  • Delete all data at any time by deleting the app or using the in-app data management tools
  • Disable iCloud sync at any time in Settings
  • Contact us at nestir@gmail.com for data-related requests

7. Children's Privacy

This App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or equivalent minimum age in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:
