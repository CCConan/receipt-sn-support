Receipt SN

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 5, 2026 | Version: v3.0

Thank you for choosing Receipt SN. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data. Please read carefully before using the app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Receipt Data: Receipt images you capture or import, along with extracted information such as merchant name, amount, date, and line items.
  • Device Information: Basic details like device model, OS version, and app version for diagnostics.
  • Error Logs: Anonymous error reports collected when the app encounters issues to help improve stability.
  • Subscription Information: Your subscription status is managed by Apple App Store. We do not store your complete payment information.

2. How We Use Your Information

Receipt SN uses your data to:

  • Provide AI recognition features to extract structured data from receipts
  • Organize and categorize your data
  • Generate statistical reports and export files
  • Diagnose and fix technical issues

3. AI and Automated Processing

Receipt SN will never use your receipt images, text, or recognition results to train AI models. Your data is used solely to provide the services you request.

When cloud-based AI services are needed for receipt recognition, images or text snippets are only processed for that single request and are not retained afterward.

4. Data Storage

Receipt SN stores your receipt images and recognition results on your local device by default. We do not actively access your private data.

If you enable iCloud backup or sync, data will be stored using Apple's infrastructure. For iCloud data handling, please refer to Apple's privacy policy.

Deleting the app removes all local data.

5. Data Security

We use encryption to protect your data, including:

  • Encrypted local data storage
  • Network transmission encryption (TLS/SSL)

Please note that no system can guarantee absolute security. To the maximum extent permitted by law, we are not liable for third-party actions or unforeseen security events.

6. Third-Party Services

Receipt SN may use the following third-party services:

  • Apple: App Store transaction verification, iCloud data sync (if enabled)
  • Cloud AI Services: For receipt recognition (processing only the current request)
  • Error Tracking Services: To improve app stability

These service providers have their own privacy policies. Please refer to their official websites for details.

7. Children's Data

Receipt SN is not designed for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You can:

  • Export your data anytime using the app's export feature
  • Delete individual receipts or all data at once
  • Remove the app to delete all local data

9. Policy Updates

We may update this policy from time to time. Continued use of Receipt SN after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.

For service usage guidelines, please refer to our Terms of Service.
