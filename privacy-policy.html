Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026 | Version: v2.2

Thank you for choosing Receipt SN. We are committed to protecting your financial privacy through a "Privacy-by-Design" architecture. This policy outlines how we handle your information and your absolute rights over your data.

Zero Human-in-the-Loop Commitment:
Receipt SN uses 100% automated AI technology. No human employees, contractors, or third-party workers ever view or manually process your receipt images or data.

1. Our Privacy Philosophy

Receipt SN empowers users with full data sovereignty. Your original financial data, receipt images, and extracted records are stored exclusively within your device's private sandbox. We do not access, monitor, or claim ownership of your data.

2. Data Collection & Usage

  • Financial Data: Compressed images and extracted transaction details are stored locally on your device.
  • Technical Analytics & Indicator Use: We collect anonymized usage metrics (e.g., device model, system version, aggregated monthly scan volume) for product optimization. We reserve the right to use aggregated, non-personally identifiable data for performance benchmarking and promotional indicators.
  • Subscription Data: Payments are processed via Apple App Store; we do not store or access your full credit card details.

3. Intelligent Processing & Global Transit

We maximize privacy through dynamic processing paths:

  • Local NPU Processing Mode: Once the Local AI package is enabled, all inference happens on-device with **Zero Data Transmission**.
  • Cloud-Assisted Processing (Dynamic Privacy Logic):
    • Standard Scripts (e.g., English): We transmit **only extracted text**; no image data is sent to the cloud.
    • Complex Scripts: For high-precision visual context requirements, compressed images may be transmitted to ensure accuracy.
    • Immediate Erasure: All cloud analysis is performed in volatile cache and is **permanently purged** immediately after extraction.

4. Zero-Training Commitment (No-Training Policy)

We solemnly affirm that Receipt SN **NEVER** uses your receipts, financial records, or personal data to train our AI models or third-party models. Your data remains your private property.

5. Security Performance & Legal Limitation

We employ multi-layer encryption for both local storage and data in transit. Users acknowledge that while we use industry-leading protective measures, no internet transmission is 100% immune. We shall not be held liable for security breaches arising from factors outside of our control (e.g., third-party device vulnerabilities or unencrypted networks).

6. Third-Party Independence

We partner only with industry leaders (Apple, Google Cloud) under strict privacy mandates. They act as independent data processors with their own policies. Receipt SN is not liable for technical failures or data breaches originating within third-party infrastructure.

7. Modifications & Control (Terms of Updates)

  • Right to Modify: We reserve the right to update this policy at any time. Significant changes will be announced via in-app notifications.
  • Continued Usage: **By continuing to use Receipt SN after an updated version of this policy is posted, you acknowledge and agree to be bound by the revised terms. If you do not agree to the new terms, you must cease use of the App immediately.**
  • Right to Erasure: You can export or delete data at any time. Deletion permanently removes records from your device and iCloud.
